Frequently Asked Questions (FAQ)

To upload your publication data, go to the "Upload Publications" section and follow the instructions provided on the page. You can upload your data in various formats, including Excel, BibTeX, or CSV.

Once you've uploaded your publication data, navigate to the "Generate Summary" section. You can customize your summary by filtering by publication type, year range, and other criteria. The generated summary will provide you with key metrics and a detailed list of your publications.

If you forget your password, click on the "Forgot Password" link on the login page. You'll be prompted to enter your email address, and a password reset link will be sent to you. Follow the instructions in the email to reset your password.
